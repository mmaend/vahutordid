About Us

The culture of sweetening the mouth of a guest is ingrained in the lifestyle of all human beings from the far reaches of the Asian continent to the tips of the South American rainforest. This principle was the founding principle of the Vahutordid where our pleasure of wowing the imagination and the taste buds lies at the core of our DNA! We live on the smiles of the satisfied! Good cakes are founded on three principles, they bring a smile, they are pure and they are tasty! We believe cakes are a must for any occasion as they add flair and grandeur to any occasion whether it’s the state of origin or origin of a state! Our aim is to ensure that every cake fulfills these principles and we are constantly striving to use the purest of ingredients and the best of techniques. At The Vahutordid you can rest assured that we will bend over backwards to ensure that you get what you want, we cater for a range of occasions from weddings to birthdays and our qualified chefs can make any cake to your specifications! We use the purest of ingredients to ensure the best of taste. Try us out today!
